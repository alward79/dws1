Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

Submitted by: Angelena

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnults (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix brown sugar and butter until blended. Beat in vanilla and egg until light and fluffy. Mix in oats, flour, and baking soda; stir in chocolate chips and chopped walnuts (optional).
  3. Drop tablespoonfuls of dough onto ungreased cookie sheet about 2 inches apart.
  4. Bake 9 - 11 minutes or until golden brown. Cool slightly and remove from cookie sheet to wire rack. Once cookes are cooled store in an airtight container.
